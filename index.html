How to play

Welcome to the Game of Thrones Word Guess Game. To start, you are given an undisclosed Game of Thrones character name to guess. Blank spaces will indicate how many letters are in the name.


Press a letter key to start. If the letter you chose is in the name, that letter will replace one or more blank spaces in the character name. If the letter is not in the name, it will appear in the Wrong Letters Guessed section below. For each incorrect guess, your guesses remaining will decrease. When guesses remaining reaches "0", the game will reset with a new character name. If you guess the name correctly, an image of the character will appear and a sound bite from Game of Thrones will play.


Good luck and enjoy the game!
